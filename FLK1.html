Mediation：调解

Questions: select the answer you consider to be correct.

Q1 : Which of the following is a primary role of the mediator?

(1)To impose a decision on the parties based on their own expertise.根据他们自己的专业知识，对当事人作出决定。

(2)To facilitate dialogue and communication among the parties in a structured, constructive way. 以有组织和建设性的方式促进各方之间的对话和沟通。

(3)To prepare the parties for litigation.为当事人准备诉讼。

(4)All of these.以上都是。

A1: the correct answer is ‘to facilitate dialogue and communication among the parties in a structured, constructive way.’The primary role of the mediator is:

(1)to facilitate dialogue and communication among the parties in a structured, constructive way以结构化，建设性的方式促进各方对话和沟通;

(2)to help the parties realistically assess their positions帮助当事人现实地评估自己的立场;

(3)to be a catalyst for a resolution designed by the parties成为各方设计决议的催化剂。

What is the aim of this module?

To help you understand how the process of Mediation works and what the outcomes of this mechanism are. 帮助你了解调解过程的工作原理以及该机制的结果

Mediation is a process that has existed for many centuries and has been used in many cultures and countries. In the past, mediators were respected community members who were known to the parties, but today mediators are merely neutrals.

Mediation can be defined as the facilitation by a third party of a negotiated agreement. The mediator does not decide the dispute, but facilitates communication and problem-solving by the parties.

Mediation can be defined as ‘the facilitation, by a third party, of a negotiated agreement… in which the mediator does not decide the dispute, but facilitates communication and problem-solving by the parties’. (调解可以被定义为“由第三方促成协商达成协议…调解员不决定争议，但促进双方的沟通和解决问题”。)

A mediator has no ‘decision-making power’. 调解员没有“决定权”

The ‘primary roles of the mediator are’: 调解员的主要角色:

(1)To facilitate dialogue and communication among the parties in a structured, constructive way; 以有组织和建设性的方式促进各方之间的对话和沟通

(2)To help the parties realistically assess their positions;帮助各方切实评估其立场;

(3)To be a catalyst for a resolution designed by the parties. 促进各方设计的解决方案。

Mediation may be performed by international organisations, by states or by individuals, and it has been used with great success to resolve a wide variety of disputes. 调解可以由国际组织，国家或个人进行，它在解决各种争议方面取得了巨大的成功。

It has proven popular for a variety of reason, including the ‘speed of procedure, the expertise of the mediators, the confidentiality of the procedure and avoidance of procedural obstacles of all sorts.’ 事实证明，它之所以受欢迎，有多种原因，包括“程序的速度，调解员的专业知识，程序的机密性以及避免了各种程序障碍”。 Although it can take place at any time in a dispute, it is more likely to be successful later on, after the parties in dispute have experienced the costs of conflict. 虽然它可以在争议的任何时候发生，但在争议各方经历了冲突的代价之后，它更有可能在后来取得成功。

Commercial Mediation商事调解

Commercial mediation begins with the signing of a confidentiality agreement. This is followed by an outline of the format, given by the mediator, and a confirmation that all of the parties have the authority to settle. 商事调解首先要签订保密协议。随后是由调解员给出的格式大纲，并确认所有当事人都有权解决。

The mediator then invites both sides to make an opening statement, which typically will include the facts as they see them and a discussion of how they have allegedly been damaged by the opposing side. 然后调解员邀请双方进行开场陈述，通常包括他们所看到的事实，并对他们依其申述是如何被对方造成损害展开讨论。

Once each party has had the opportunity to present its case, the mediator will summarise the information before hosting joint or private meetings with the parties.一旦各方都

Each party then will discuss its position with the mediator-describing its view of the core issues and what it ultimately seeks as an outcome.然后，各方将与调解员讨论其立场-描述其对核心问题的看法以及最终寻求的结果。

Roles of the mediator

For the mediation to succeed, the parties must not only have confidence in the skill and discretion of the mediator but, in addition, must feel free to allow the mediator to privately explore their strengths and weakness with regard to the opposing party, with respect to factual and legal issues. This is why many parties will support private meetings. 为了使调解成功，当事人不仅必须对调解员的技巧和判断力有信心，而且还必须自由地允许调解员就事实和法律问题私下探讨他们于对方而言的优势和弱势。这就是为什么很多当事人会支持非公开会议。

The mediator should then work to bring about a settlement which satisfies both parties’ interest. Therefore, a solution in the case of mediation can only result from an agreement reached by both parties. 然后，调解员应努力达成满足双方利益的解决方案。因此，调解只能由双方当事人协商一致才能解决。
